MazeBall Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Publisher: Mayaverse Labs (OPC) Private Limited

App package: com.mayaverselabs.mazeball

MazeBall does not require an account or registration. MazeBall does not display ads and does not use analytics or tracking. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

MazeBall is an offline live wallpaper. The app does not declare the Android Internet permission and does not transmit data over a network. Accelerometer readings are processed temporarily on the device to control the ball. Accelerometer readings are not stored or transmitted.

Game progress, preferences, and local statistics are stored only in app-private local storage on the device. Android cloud backup and device-to-device transfer are disabled for MazeBall app data.

Selecting Rate MazeBall opens Google Play only after the user explicitly taps that action.

Users can remove local data by clearing MazeBall app storage in Android Settings or by uninstalling the app, subject to Android platform behaviour.
