TiltScapes Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 July 2026 · App package: com.mayaverselabs.tiltscapes

Publisher: Mayaverse Labs (OPC) Private Limited

TiltScapes is an offline Android live-wallpaper app. It does not require an account or registration and does not display ads.

Data collection and sharing

TiltScapes does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information or device data. The app does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs and does not declare the Android Internet permission.

Motion sensor data

The app reads the device rotation-vector sensor only to calculate the wallpaper's parallax movement. Sensor readings are processed temporarily on the device while the wallpaper is visible. They are not stored, linked to an identity, or transmitted.

Information stored on the device

TiltScapes stores only app preferences in Android app-private storage, such as the selected scene, parallax intensity, frame-rate choice, ambient-animation setting, and Battery Saver preference. These settings may be included in Android's standard encrypted cloud backup or device-to-device transfer, depending on the user's Android and Google backup settings. Mayaverse Labs cannot access this backup data.

Deleting local data

Users can remove TiltScapes settings by clearing the app's storage in Android Settings or by uninstalling the app, subject to Android platform and backup behaviour.

Children

TiltScapes does not knowingly collect data from children or from any other user. The app is not specifically designed or marketed for children.

Changes to this policy

If TiltScapes later adds features that affect data handling, this policy and the app's Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated before that version is released.
