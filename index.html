FillUp Reminder — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 July 2026 · App package: com.sensei.pour

FillUp Reminder is a local-first Android live-wallpaper and routine-reminder app. The short version: your data stays on your device, and the developer collects nothing.

No account, no collection

  • The app requires no account, sign-in, or registration.
  • The developer does not intentionally collect, receive, transmit, sell, rent, or share any user data. The app declares no INTERNET permission, so it has no network access at the operating-system level.
  • There are no ads, no analytics, no crash-reporting SDKs, no tracking or attribution SDKs, no advertising-identifier use, no cloud sync, no remote configuration, and no developer-accessible telemetry.

What stays on your device

The app stores your reminders (times, repeat windows, and labels you type), saved routines, chosen themes and animation settings, and basic app state — only in local app storage. This data is excluded from Android cloud backup and from Android 12+ device-to-device transfer, so it never leaves your device via any Android backup mechanism.

Device features the app uses, and why

  • Notifications (optional): reminder alerts with snooze/apply actions. Requested only after you create or enable a reminder — never at app startup. Declining keeps the wallpaper and schedule working.
  • Exact alarms (optional special access): if you allow "Alarms & reminders" in Android Settings, reminders fire at the exact time; otherwise Android may deliver them a few minutes late. The app never opens this settings screen by itself.
  • Motion/accelerometer sensor: processed entirely on the device, only to animate the liquid tilt effect. Never stored or transmitted.
  • Boot completed: re-arms your enabled reminders after a restart.
  • Live wallpaper & widget system APIs: render the wallpaper you selected and show your next reminder. Android always asks you to confirm a live-wallpaper change; the app cannot change your wallpaper silently.
  • Vibration: haptics on reminder notifications.

Children

The app is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children.

Not a medical device

FillUp Reminder is a general routine and personalization tool. It is not a medical device or healthcare service, makes no treatment or health-outcome claims, and should not be your only method of remembering critical medication or treatment.

Your controls

  • Remove all app data: Android Settings → Apps → FillUp Reminder → Storage → Clear storage, or uninstall the app. Nothing is stored off-device, so this removes everything.
  • Disable notifications, exact-alarm access, or the live wallpaper at any time in Android Settings.

Changes to this policy

If a future version introduces analytics, advertising, purchases/billing, or any cloud or network feature, this policy and the Google Play Data Safety declaration will be updated before that version is released.
